Syracuse University, Fall 2025

Looking ahead into the tree plantings of the NYC boroughs, the Bronx will see more than 1,000 tree plantings in the Decmeber 2025 season. This is around a 50% increase from the previous planting season in August of 2025.

In the January 2026 planting season, no tree plantings are planned to occur in Manhattan or Staten Island. However, there are over 2,500 tree plantings planned in Queens.

In the June 2026 planting season there are six tree plantings planned, of these four will be in the city council district six, with two planned to be on West End Ave and two planned to be on Columbus Ave.
