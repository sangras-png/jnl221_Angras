SIYA ANGRAS

Syracuse University, Fall 2025

Food service establishments in the City of Syracuse have more total violation numbers as compared to other cities in Onandaga County. This hypothesis was made because the City of Syracuse is a metropolitan area as opposed to suburban places that are in the dataset. The City of Syracuse has a total of around 450 resteraunts, which is more than the rest of Onandaga County. Although the city of Syracuse also has a high population, the city has a lot of foodspots that Syracuse University students and others go to dine at.
